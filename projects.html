Urban Divas ~since 2007~

One of the pioneers of Slovenian electronic music is guided by her passion for Techno - not only through numerous DJ performances but also as an organiser of events (including the first techno events in Slovenia in general) and a promoter of women's DJing and producing. As one of the few female DJs in Slovenia she helps young women to develop their personal & professional talent.

In 2007 Trick-C started the project Urban Divas - it's the name of her events and a socially engaged international initiative for promotion and development of talented women in electronic music with which she wants to provide additional feminine touch. Much attention is given to less renowned girls, enabling them the opportunity she didn't have.

Urban Divas is a project created after many years of experience in the field of organisation, booking and participation in various projects on the basis of a number of experiences and cooperation in the promotion of female DJs in Slovenia, neighbouring Croatia and Belgium.

Among the first Urban Divas beside Trick-C were - Natalija Bogataj / House Wife, one of the most sophisticated underground DJanes with a long mileage and still active in the Slovenian underworld; young Marina Karamarko (ex. Miss Soulfly), today resident for the VIVa Warriors parties at Sankeys and Sankeys Global resident DJ and the "First Lady of Croatian Electronic Music" - Virgin Helena.

In 10 years Urban Divas hosted and shared the stage with many women in electronic music. Lory The Hi Fi Princess (CAN), Beyza (TR) Miss Sunshine aka Insolate (HR), Mistress Barbara (S), Gayle Sun (J), Nakadia (THAI), La Di Da (CZ), Miss Djax (NL), Linda Pearl (GER), Miss Electric (AUT), Electric Indigo (AUT), Daniela Haverbeck (NL), Juliana Yamasaki & Julyukie (BR / GER).

Today the residence at Urban Divas (and Bodywork) belongs besides Trick-C to a young, talented lady from Zagreb /Croatia - Teychee (Insurgency). Urban Divas is a synonym for female touch on the dance floor but expect uncompromised Techno, old and new school, hard, deep, dark & driving BPM's of Urban Ladies.

BodyWork ~since 2012~

The Pioneer of Slovenian electronic movement and renowned club promoter Trick-C has come up with a new series of intimate and at the same time special events called BodyWork.

The concept preserves the pristine touch of electronic events, where the most important thing is gathering of "young" and "young by heart" with the quality music of selected domestic and foreign artists of underground techno .

BodyWork puts "Underground Techno" into a wider club environment. Thus it brings freshness and novelty... today's techno sound under the fingers of skilled selectors is such that it quickly clings under the skin of all generations, who are constantly looking for dynamics, power and energy in music.

For all people who love techno with the touch of the pristine underground.
